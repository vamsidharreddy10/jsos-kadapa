Volunteer FAQs

Answers to common questions about volunteering

What are the requirements to volunteer?

Most roles require no specific qualifications beyond being 18+ years old (16+ for some roles with guardian consent). We provide all necessary training. Some specialized roles may require relevant experience or certifications.

How much time do I need to commit?

Time commitments vary by role. Some positions require as little as 2-4 hours per week, while others may need more regular availability. We have opportunities for both short-term and long-term volunteers.

Will I receive training?

Yes! All volunteers receive orientation and role-specific training. We also offer ongoing support and skill development workshops throughout your volunteering journey.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Some roles (like content creation, IT support, and virtual tutoring) can be done remotely. Other positions require in-person participation at our centers or in communities.

Do you accept student volunteers?

Absolutely! We welcome student volunteers and can provide documentation for service learning or internship credit when needed.
