Our Journey

Key milestones that shaped JSOS

2005

The Beginning

Founded in a small community center with just 5 volunteers, JSOS began as an after-school tutoring program for 30 children.

2010

First Expansion

Opened three new learning centers, reaching over 500 students annually. Launched our scholarship program.

2015

National Recognition

Received the National Education Award for innovative programs. Partnered with 10 local schools.

2020

Digital Transformation

Launched online learning platform during pandemic, serving 5,000+ students remotely.
