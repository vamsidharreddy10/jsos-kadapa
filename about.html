Our Story

How JSOS began and our journey so far

2010

Foundation

JSOS was founded by a group of passionate individuals who wanted to make a difference in their community. Starting with just a small team of 5 volunteers.

2013

First Major Project

Launched our Education for All initiative, providing school supplies and tutoring to 200 children in underserved communities.

2018

National Recognition

Received the National Community Service Award for our innovative approach to solving local problems.

2023

Expanding Reach

Now serving over 10,000 beneficiaries annually across 5 regions with a team of 50 staff and 300 volunteers.

Our Mission

To empower communities through sustainable development initiatives, education programs, and by creating opportunities for those in need. We believe in a world where everyone has access to the resources they need to thrive.

Our Vision

A society where inequality is eliminated, where every individual has equal opportunities to succeed, and where communities work together to create lasting positive change.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Compassion

We approach every challenge with empathy and understanding for those we serve.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our actions and decisions.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnership and community engagement.

Innovation

We constantly seek creative solutions to complex social problems.

Sustainability

Our programs are designed to create lasting impact beyond our direct involvement.

Accountability

We take responsibility for our actions and are transparent in our operations.
